Chai Recipe

    ingredients

  1. water
  2. tea leaves
  3. milk
  4. cardomn
  5. black pepper
  6. ginger
  7. sugar

    Process


  • firstly on the gas stove
  • put a vessel on it, along with water in it.
  • Put tea leaves into it according to the person you are making for
  • grate the ginger,cardmon,and black pepper
  • put all the grated things into the vessel
  • after a boil ,put sugar into it.
  • after again a boil,finally put milk into it
  • stain it into tea pot
  • serve hot cup of tea
